This Privacy Policy explains how Crown AI ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use the AI Study OS mobile application. By using the app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1 Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve AI Study OS:

CategoryData CollectedPurpose
Account Info Google name, email address, profile picture Authentication and personalisation
Study Content Chat history, questions asked, AI responses Provide AI tutoring service
Notes & Materials Saved notes, study plans, uploaded photos Sync notes across sessions
Progress Data Quiz results, scores, streaks, session history Analytics and recommendations

We do not collect passwords, financial card details, or location data.

2 How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively to deliver and improve the AI Study OS experience:

  • Provide AI responses — Your questions are sent to GroqCloud AI to generate answers.
  • Personalise your experience — Recommendations, subject tracking, and progress analytics.
  • Sync across sessions — Your notes, plans, and history are stored so you can continue where you left off.
  • Manage your subscription — Track plan status, billing cycle, and unlock features accordingly.
  • Send notifications — Reminders, streak alerts, and important app updates.
  • Debug and improve — Crash reports and usage patterns help us fix bugs and build better features.

🔒 We never sell your personal data to third parties. Your information is used only to operate AI Study OS.

3 Third-Party Services

AI Study OS integrates with the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

🔐 Google Sign-In (Alphabet Inc.)

Used for authentication. Google receives your login request; we receive your name, email, and profile picture. Google Privacy Policy →

🤖 GroqCloud AI (Groq, Inc.)

Your questions and conversation context are sent to Groq's servers to generate AI responses. Groq processes this data per their privacy policy. We do not send personally identifiable information in AI prompts.

🗄️ Railway PostgreSQL (Railway Corp.)

Your account data, chat history, notes, and study progress are stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted on Railway's secure infrastructure.

⚡ Redis (Railway-hosted)

Used for session caching and temporary rate-limiting data. No personal data is permanently stored in Redis.

💳 Google Play Billing (Alphabet Inc.)

All subscription payments are processed by Google Play. We receive only your subscription status (plan type, billing cycle) — we never see your payment card details. Google Payments Privacy →

🔔 Firebase Cloud Messaging (Alphabet Inc.)

Used to deliver push notifications to your device. Firebase stores your device token for notification routing. Firebase Privacy Policy →

4 Data Storage & Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data:

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS encrypted connections.
  • Passwords are never stored — authentication is handled by Google Sign-In.
  • Database access is restricted to authorised services only.
  • API keys and credentials are stored securely in environment variables, never in the app code.
  • Your data is stored on servers located in secure cloud infrastructure (Railway, USA).

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong Google account password and enable two-factor authentication.

5 Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active:

  • Account data (name, email) — Retained until account deletion.
  • Chat history & notes — Retained until deleted by you or on account deletion.
  • Analytics data — Aggregated usage statistics may be retained in anonymised form after account deletion.
  • Payment records — Subscription history is retained as required by applicable law and Google Play's policies.

When you delete your account, all personally identifiable data is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

6 Your Rights

  • Correction — Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Deletion — Request deletion of your account and all associated data ("Right to be Forgotten").
  • Data Portability — Request an export of your data in a readable format.
  • Opt-out of notifications — Disable push notifications at any time from your device settings or within the app.

7 Children's Privacy

AI Study OS is designed for educational use by students of all ages. However, our service is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13.

  • Children under 13 should only use AI Study OS with verifiable parental or guardian consent.
  • If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information without your consent, please contact us immediately.
  • We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 without parental consent. If we become aware of such data, we will delete it promptly.
